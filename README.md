In today’s fast-evolving world, students and professionals often struggle to find the right opportunities—whether internships, jobs, scholarships, or skill-building resources—aligned with their unique skills, interests, and career goals.

Existing job and opportunity platforms provide generic recommendations that often fail to address individual needs. Users spend significant time browsing irrelevant listings, leading to missed opportunities, frustration, and inefficiency.

There is a clear need for a personalized platform that can:

Understand user skills, interests, and preferences.

Recommend relevant opportunities in real-time.

Provide an organized dashboard to manage saved and applied opportunities.

Help students and professionals make better career decisions with minimal effort.

CareerBridge aims to solve this problem by creating a smart, user-friendly system that delivers tailored recommendations, saves time, and increases the chances of success for its users.


CareerBridge is a modern Internship & Career Portal built to solve the biggest problems faced by students and professionals: spam opportunities, scattered platforms, irrelevant jobs, and lack of personalized career guidance.

It provides a unified dashboard where users can explore verified opportunities, discover skill gaps, follow a personalized roadmap, and track their career progress — all in one place.

🚀 Key Features

✨ What makes CareerBridge different:

🎯 Personalized & Verified Opportunities
AI-driven matching ensures you only see genuine and profile-fit internships.

📊 Skill Gap Insights & Career Roadmaps
Identify missing skills (High/Medium/Low priority) and follow step-by-step guidance.

📌 Unified Dashboard & Profile Management
Manage internships, insights, and your profile in one organized platform.

🔔 Progress Tracking & Notifications
Weekly growth reports and timely alerts so you never miss a deadline.

🛠 Tech Stack

Frontend: React.js + Vite + CSS

Backend (Planned): Node.js + Express

Database (Planned): MongoDB / PostgreSQL

AI/ML (Planned): Python (Scikit-learn, NLP)

🚀 Features

🔑 User Authentication – Sign up, login, and manage profiles securely.

📝 Profile Management – Add skills, interests, education, and experience.

🎯 Personalized Recommendations – Smart matching of users with opportunities.

🔍 Search & Filters – Easily search and filter opportunities by category, location, or type.

⭐ Save & Apply – Bookmark opportunities or apply directly.

📊 Dashboard – Track applications and saved opportunities.

🔔 Notifications – Get alerts for newly matched opportunities.



📝 Steps to Use

Sign Up / Log In

Create an account using email/Google.

Log in with your credentials.

Complete Your Profile

Add your skills, interests, education, and career goals.

Upload resume (optional) for better recommendations.

Explore Opportunities

Browse the opportunity feed (internships, jobs, scholarships, events, etc.).

Use search and filters (location, category, type) to narrow down results.

Get Personalized Recommendations

Based on your profile, the system suggests tailored opportunities.

Recommendations update dynamically as you add new skills or goals.

Save or Apply

Save opportunities to your dashboard for later.

Apply directly through links provided in each opportunity.

Track Your Progress

View your saved and applied opportunities in the dashboard.

Monitor your application history and upcoming deadlines.

Stay Updated

Get notifications when new opportunities match your profile.

Keep your profile updated to improve accuracy of recommendations.
